Our History

TechCon began as a small local meetup in 2010, bringing together passionate developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into an international event showcasing breakthrough innovations and fostering a strong sense of community in the tech world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower creators, thinkers, and innovators by providing a space for collaboration, learning, and inspiration. We believe in diversity, accessibility, and building a future where technology benefits everyone.

Notable Past Speakers

Rose Knowles

Rose is a software engineer and AI researcher who spoke at TechCon 2021 about ethical AI and its role in shaping tomorrow's innovations.

Circe Coulter

Circe, founder of DevWorks Africa, joined TechCon 2022 to discuss inclusive tech ecosystems and building with communities in mind.

Chidera Onuoha

Chidera is a cybersecurity expert who spoke in 2023 about digital privacy and protecting users in a connected world.
